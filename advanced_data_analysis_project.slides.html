# set individual bar lables using above list

To summer up¶

As for dog's stages,

Dogs of " Pupper " stage has large fans base, but it still doesn't lead the counts compared to other stages, and may be its large fan base comes from factors other than thoses available in the data, (favorites counts, retweets counts or ratings). Eventhough "Rating" has no availabe enough data to define the word.

As for prediction methods,

Although second prediction method has higher correct predictions, but first prediction method yet stands as the most reliable one depending on its confirmation percentage to number of correctly predicted images. Favorites and retweets counts and dogs' stages doesn't show considerable effect on prediction accuracy
